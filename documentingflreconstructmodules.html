Introduction

As we saw in the tutorial on writing custom modules for FLReconstruct, it is easy to make parameters of the module configurable at run time through the ASCII text datatools::multi_properties pipeline script. However, this creates an issue for the user of your module in finding out what parameters your module exposes for configuration, what their purpose is, and what types/values they may take. In this tutorial we show how to integrate your module with the datatools::object_configuration_description system.
